International Yoga Day

Breathe in, Breathe out

DATE: 21 June, 2018 (Thursday)

DURATION: 10:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.

VENUE: SAMVAD, FR.CRCE, BANDSTAND, BANDRA (W)

The event began with the Public Relations Head Miss Sanjana Mahadik giving a brief introduction about yoga and introducing the yoga instructor Mrs. Samruddhi Tandel and her assistants. The students' council then welcomed her with a bouquet of flowers. All the people present got the chance to know how yoga embodies unity of mind and body. Different yoga postures were explained and demonstrated by Mrs. Samruddhi Joshi like:- Kati Sanchaalan, Ghutna sanchalan, Thadasan, Vrukshasan, Padh-hasthasan, Ardhchakrasan, Pawana Muktaasana. The programme ended with Pranayama and meditation prayers were recited before and after the programme. The staff and the students had a peaceful session. The audience showed great interest and participated in various asanas with full cooperation.

The event was concluded with the council presenting Mrs. Samruddhi Tandel with a small memento and a small talk by the Principal.
